Discover India

A Journey Through Tradition and Innovation

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the vibrant tapestry of India, where ancient traditions blend harmoniously with modern innovations. From the majestic palaces of Rajasthan to the tranquil backwaters of Kerala, each destination beckons with its own unique charm and allure. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage, savor the tantalizing flavors of Indian cuisine, and marvel at the breathtaking landscapes that unfold before you. With our expertly crafted itinerary, you'll uncover the many wonders of India and create memories to last a lifetime.

itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

  • Welcome to Delhi, the bustling capital city of India
  • Transfer to your hotel and check-in
  • Evening at leisure to explore the vibrant markets and bustling streets of Old Delhi

Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing

  • Visit iconic landmarks including the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, and Chandni Chowk
  • Explore the historic Qutub Minar and Humayun's Tomb, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Drive past the India Gate and Parliament House
  • Evening at leisure to relax or explore the city further

Day 3: Agra - Taj Mahal Excursion

  • Early morning drive to Agra, home to the magnificent Taj Mahal
  • Visit the Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World, and marvel at its breathtaking beauty
  • Explore the Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and learn about its rich history
  • Return to Delhi in the evening and overnight stay

Day 4: Jaipur - The Pink City

  • Travel to Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, known as the Pink City
  • Visit the majestic Amber Fort and enjoy an elephant ride to the hilltop fortress
  • Explore the City Palace and Jantar Mantar Observatory
  • Stroll through the colorful markets of Jaipur and shop for souvenirs
  • Overnight stay in Jaipur

Day 5: Jaipur Sightseeing

  • Visit the Hawa Mahal, or Palace of Winds, known for its intricate latticework
  • Explore the picturesque Jal Mahal, a water palace situated amidst the Man Sagar Lake
  • Optional visit to the nearby Nahargarh Fort for panoramic views of the city
  • Evening at leisure to relax or explore Jaipur's vibrant nightlife

Day 6: Udaipur Sightseeing

  • Visit the Jagdish Temple, a fine example of Indo-Aryan architecture
  • Explore the Shilpgram Craft Village, showcasing traditional Rajasthani arts and crafts
  • Optional visit to the Monsoon Palace for panoramic views of the city
  • Evening at leisure to explore the local markets or enjoy a cultural performance
  • Farewell dinner with traditional Rajasthani cuisine

Day 7: Departure from Udaipur

  • Transfer to the airport for your onward journey or return home with cherished memories of your India tour.
